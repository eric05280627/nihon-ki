To anyone who happens upon these writings:

This is a story set within the same universe as *Nausicaä of the Valley of the Wind*, chronicling the history of Japan and Northeast Asia from the 41st century onward. Through this narrative, I explore the cycles of civilizational collapse and reconstruction, and the divergent paths of humanity and culture.

This is not a "normal" or "entertaining" story in the conventional sense. It is written in Classical Chinese (Hanwen)—a style that is dense, demanding, and perhaps obscure. While I acknowledge these texts may be perceived as mediocre by some, they contain themes I deem profound, and I cannot bear to let them be buried with me.

If you find yourself truly interested, I am deeply grateful for your patience and your time. It is my hope that these words might offer you some measure of meaningful reflection.
